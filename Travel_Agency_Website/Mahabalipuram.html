Mahabalipuram

Mahabalipuram, a coastal town in Tamil Nadu, India, is a treasure trove of ancient history and architectural marvels.

Enchanting Mahabalipuram Retreat

Dive into the Serenity of Mahabalipuram with Our Enchanting Retreat Package!

Day 1: Arrival in Mahabalipuram

Arrive in Mahabalipuram and check into a charming resort nestled in the lush greenery. After some relaxation, explore the nearby tea gardens and enjoy a serene evening walk. Dinner at the resort's restaurant.

Day 2: Mahabalipuram Sightseeing

Morning visit to the Tea Museum to learn about the tea-making process. Proceed to Mattupetty Dam for a boat ride and breathtaking views of the surrounding hills. Visit the Echo Point to experience the unique natural echo phenomenon. Afternoon visit to the Kundala Lake and the beautiful Top Station for panoramic views. Return to the resort for a cozy dinner.

Day 3: Nature Walk and Departure

Morning nature walk through the scenic landscapes and forests. Check out from the resort and visit the famous Eravikulam National Park to spot Nilgiri Tahr. Explore the picturesque Anamudi Peak, the highest peak in South India. Departure with wonderful memories of Mahabalipuram.

Package Inclusions:

Accommodation for 2 nights in a well-appointed room. Daily breakfast and dinner at the resort. Transfers for sightseeing as per the itinerary. Boat ride at Mattupetty Dam. Entry fees to Tea Museum, Mattupetty Dam, and Eravikulam National Park. Guided nature walk.

Package Exclusions:

Transportation to and from Mahabalipuram. Lunch and other personal expenses. Any additional activities not mentioned in the itinerary.
